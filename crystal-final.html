About

I'm 曾银晶 (Crystal Zeng), a Marketing student at Guangdong University of Foreign Studies (2024—2028), with a GPA of 3.76/4.0 (top 15%) and a scholarship recipient.

As an AIGC content creator and cultural IP designer, I believe design is not just visual presentation — it's a vehicle for storytelling. Through prompt engineering, multimodal pipeline design, and AI toolchains, I bring traditional culture to life in the digital world: seen, remembered, and loved.

My flagship project, 「影灵灵 · AI Shadow Puppet Cultural IP」, covers the full AIGC production pipeline from character design to original Bilibili short films. I also built a Feishu Automation System that compressed response time from 30 minutes to 5 seconds using zero-code automation.

Previously interned at China Foreign Trade Guangzhou Property Development Co., Ltd., handling cultural product data analysis and client engagement. I'm always exploring the intersection of traditional culture, AI technology, and modern marketing.
